Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn coins on Earnzy?
You can earn coins by watching ads, completing tasks, scratching cards, daily check-ins, opening treasure boxes, and referring friends. Each activity gives you a specific amount of coins.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount?
The minimum withdrawal amount is 1,000 coins. There is a 5% processing fee on all withdrawals.
How long does it take to receive my withdrawal?
Withdrawals are typically processed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification once your withdrawal has been processed.
Can I use multiple devices with my account?
Yes, you can use your account on multiple devices. However, you cannot earn coins simultaneously on multiple devices for the same activity.
What happens if I forget my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
How does the referral program work?
Share your unique referral code with friends. When they sign up using your code and complete their first task, both you and your friend receive 250 coins as a bonus.
